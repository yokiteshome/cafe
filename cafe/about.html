About Kiya cafe and Restaurant

At Kiya cafe and Restaurant, we honor Habesha traditions, brewing buna in the Ethiopian coffee ceremony and preparing fresh injera and richly spiced wot with love, in a warm and welcoming space. A place where great taste meets great atmosphere.

Whether you're pausing after a buna ceremony brewed in a jebena, sharing injera with wot and tibs, or meeting friends to the aroma of Ethiopian spices and incense, we celebrate Habesha warmth and make every moment feel like home.

Our journey is rooted in the Habesha buna tradition: from handroasting beans to the aroma of incense during the ceremony. We source heirloom Ethiopian coffees from regions like Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Harar, roast in small batches, and brew with care, whether in a jebena, pourover, or espresso.

We honor community and warm hospitality. Come sip buna, share a gursha, and feel at home. Welcome!

A Great place to enjoy the being ethiopian!
